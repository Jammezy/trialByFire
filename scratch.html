    • Thou Mayest Freely Eat


    Among the various food possibilities, the following are generally considered the main plant-based food groups

    1. Fruits
    2. Vegetables
    3. Whole Grains
    4. Legumes
      • Beans
      • Peas
      • Lentils
    5. Nuts and Seeds

    These food groups are the basis of a healthy plant-based diet. They are rich in nutrients, fiber, and antioxidants, and can help reduce the risk of chronic diseases.

    Eating a variety of foods from these groups can help ensure that individuals get all the nutrients needed for optimal health.
